Health Tips & Medication Guidelines

General Medication Safety

  • Always consult a doctor before starting any medication
  • Follow prescribed dosages exactly as recommended
  • Complete antibiotic courses even if you feel better
  • Store medicines properly in cool, dry places away from sunlight
  • Check expiration dates before consuming any medication
  • Never share prescription medicines with others
  • Report side effects to your healthcare provider immediately

When to Seek Immediate Medical Attention

  • Difficulty breathing or chest pain
  • Severe allergic reactions (swelling, rash, difficulty swallowing)
  • High fever (>103Â°F or 39.4Â°C) that doesn't respond to medication
  • Persistent vomiting or diarrhea leading to dehydration
  • Sudden severe headache or vision changes
  • Signs of internal bleeding or severe injury

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise regularly (at least 30 minutes daily)
  • Stay hydrated (8-10 glasses of water daily)
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours for adults)
  • Manage stress through meditation or yoga
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption
  • Regular health check-ups and screenings

Preventive Healthcare

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water
  • Keep vaccinations up to date
  • Practice good hygiene and sanitation
  • Wear masks in crowded or polluted areas
  • Maintain social distancing during disease outbreaks
  • Boost immunity with vitamin-rich foods

Important Warnings

  • Do not self-medicate for serious conditions
  • Avoid mixing medications without doctor's approval
  • No alcohol with most medications
  • Pregnant/breastfeeding women must consult before any medication
  • Children's dosages differ significantly from adults
